About AgriVision+

Welcome to AgriVision+ — a modern crop disease detection platform powered by AI and built for farmers, agricultural workers, and researchers.

We are a team of passionate developers, data scientists, and agricultural experts working together to bring smart technology to rural farming communities.

Our goal is to help farmers detect plant diseases early and take quick action — increasing crop yield, reducing losses, and improving food security.
